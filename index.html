Our Story

A gym built around community & connection.

Goodbrand Fitness was created so that women could have a safe space to feel accepted and supported while achieving their fitness goals. Whether you're here for healthy weight loss, getting fit, or relieving stress — our encouraging and inclusive community will help you feel stronger than ever, both physically and mentally.

We offer group fitness classes in-person at our South Surrey studio, live-streamed on Zoom, or as recorded workouts you can do on your schedule. Plus customized nutrition guides, our members-only community, and ongoing trainer support.
